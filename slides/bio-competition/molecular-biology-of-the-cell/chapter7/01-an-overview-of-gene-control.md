# An Overview of Gene Control

## 基因调控概述

---

## Same DNA, Different Cells

- **Key Finding**: Different cell types contain the same DNA but express different sets of RNAs and proteins
- **Classic Evidence**: 
  - Frog nuclear transplantation experiments (Gurdon, 1968)
  - Plant tissue culture and regeneration
  - Mammalian cloning (Dolly the sheep)

## Gene Expression Patterns

### Common Features Across All Cells
- Structural proteins of chromosomes
- RNA and DNA polymerases
- DNA repair enzymes
- Ribosomal proteins and RNAs
- Central metabolic enzymes
- Cytoskeletal proteins (e.g., actin)

### Cell-Type Specific Expression
- **Hemoglobin**: Expressed only in red blood cells
- **Tyrosine aminotransferase**: Highly expressed in liver cells
- **RNA-seq data**: Shows 30-60% of ~25,000 human genes are expressed in any given cell type
