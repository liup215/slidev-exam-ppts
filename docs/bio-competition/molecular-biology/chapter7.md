## Study Notes

### Important Concepts

1. **Combinatorial Control**: Multiple transcription factors work together to regulate gene expression

2. **Epigenetics**: Heritable changes in gene expression that don't involve DNA sequence changes

3. **RNA Interference**: A mechanism for post-transcriptional gene silencing

### Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Transcription factor | Protein that binds to DNA and regulates transcription |
| Enhancer | DNA sequence that increases transcription |
| Silencer | DNA sequence that decreases transcription |
| Chromatin | Complex of DNA and proteins in the nucleus |
| Epigenetics | Heritable changes in gene expression |
